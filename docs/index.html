About Me

As a dedicated Natural Language Processing Engineer with 2 years of experience, I specialize in developing and optimizing Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) and Text-to-Speech (TTS) systems.

My expertise includes authoring research papers focused on advancements in TTS and ASR technologies, implementing state-of-the-art models, and managing large-scale audio datasets exceeding 300,000 hours. I'm proficient in optimization tasks integral to machine learning operations, including using Kubeflow and Jenkins pipelines for efficient ML workflows.

My engineering background from the Military University of Technology provides a strong foundation to navigate and innovate within the complex landscape of NLP and machine learning engineering. My collaborative spirit and dedication to pushing the boundaries of technology drive my continuous pursuit of excellence in the dynamic field of NLP.

Projects

VALL-E X Implementation (Voice Cloning)

Implemented a paper-based TTS model for voice cloning based on VALL-E X architecture. Created a custom PyTorch implementation with techniques to speed up training and inference, including flash attention and key-value caching.

PyTorch Transformers TTS Voice Cloning

Multilingual ASR System

Developed on-device production ASR for European languages and created a proof of concept for multilingual ASR. Built scripts for training and inference using transformer-based models with distributed data-parallel training on multiple GPUs.

PyTorch Conformer ASR DDP

Voicebox Implementation

Developed and experimented with a flow matching-based model for voice cloning. Achieved close to SOTA results in voice cloning tasks for high, medium, and low resource languages (German, Spanish, Polish, Romanian, Swedish, Korean).

PyTorch Flow Matching TTS Multilingual
